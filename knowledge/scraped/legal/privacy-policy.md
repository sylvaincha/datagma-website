	PRIVACY POLICY
DATAGMA
 
LAST UPDATE:  10 march 2025
 
By browsing our websites, subscribing to our services, creating a user account, or downloading our extension, you may be required to provide Datagma (hereinafter referred to as “Datagma" or "we") with your personal data concerning you.
 
We kindly ask you to review this policy, which explains how we process and protect your personal data in compliance with the applicable regulations, including, in particular, the European General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (hereinafter the "GDPR"). 
 

Data controller

 
Depending on the context, Datagma acts either as data controller or as data processor.
Datagma acts as data controller when collecting and using personal data of its prospects, clients and visitors of its websites.
For any question relating to your personal data, you may contact Datagma, as data controller, at the following addresses: 
 

By e-mail, at:  [•]gdpr@datagma.com ; or 
By postal mail at: Informations, 3 bld de Sebastopol, 75001, Paris, France.

Datagma acts as data processor when collecting and using personal data on behalf of its clients, including with respect to the opt-out form provided by Datagma on its website. When Datagma’s clients use its B2B data enrichment services, they act as data controllers and are responsible for ensuring that personal information collected about data subjects is being processed lawfully, fairly and in a transparent manner.
When acting as data processor, Datagma processes identification data, contact data and professional data (for instance, organization, position, or seniority) of data subjects targeted by its clients. Such data is either directly provided by the data subject or collected from trusted business partners. Datagma does not seek to collect any non-business-related data and does not collect any sensitive personal data.
Opt-out data are retained for at least 3 years to ensure the effectivity of the opt-out, in compliance with the recommendations of the French data protection authority.
Datagma’s clients use Datagma’s B2B data enrichment services for marketing and recruitment purposes to serve their legitimate interest to obtain up-to-date and complete information about potential business partners, customers or employees.
